What’s new for 2019.

Supplemental Life and AD&D Insurance Premiums

Premiums are increasing for accident and supplemental life insurance for you, and for life insurance for your spouse or domestic partner. Please take time during Open Enrollment to evaluate how much life insurance coverage is right for you. It may be that Apple already provides all the coverage you need. Learn more

Health Insurance Premiums

Health care premiums are increasing for most plans due to the rising costs of treatment and advanced technologies. For all plans, the increase is less than $10 a pay period. View premiums

Save more through your Health Savings Account

The annual HSA contribution limit is going up to $3500 for individuals and $7000 for family coverage — which means you can save more with a HSA in 2019. View Health Savings Accounts
